Conclusion

During the pandemic we saw a massive surge of nostalgia content flood the entertainment industry. Club penguin rewritten, Disney’s star wars, Netflix’s streaming content, and Nintendo’s Pokemon were some of the most notable instances of nostalgia marketing over the lockdown. While these ventures were all successful before the pandemic, they each benefitted over the lockdown because people had nothing to do but reminisce over past good times. It is tough to say if this marketing strategy will be successful in the far future, however we can likely expect that over the next 3 years, a large portion of content from the entertainment industry will be filled with nostalgic content.
